Workouts

  • Arms

    This workout focuses on building strength and size in the arms. It includes exercises such as bicep curls, tricep dips, and hammer curls.

  • Back

    This workout targets the muscles in the back, helping to develop a strong and defined upper body. It includes exercises like deadlifts, rows, and pull-ups.

  • Legs

    This leg workout is designed to strengthen and tone the lower body. It includes exercises such as squats, lunges, and leg presses.

  • Chest

    This workout focuses on building a strong and well-defined chest. It includes exercises like bench press, chest flyes, and push-ups.

  • Core

    This core workout targets the abdominal muscles, helping to improve core strength and stability. It includes exercises such as planks, crunches, and Russian twists.

  • Push Pull Legs Split

    This workout program is based on a push-pull-legs training split, which involves separating exercises based on the movement pattern. It provides a balanced approach to training different muscle groups.
